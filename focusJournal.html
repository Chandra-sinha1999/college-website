The content of this journal shall focus on to provide an open forum for researchers and academicians to come up with researches in their field. Its main focus will be on research and higher education. It will allow researchers to keep up to date with the developments of their field and direct their own research. It will aim at providing state-of-the-art knowledge in all areas pertinent to science, technology and management and become a platform for instructive and collaborative research.
